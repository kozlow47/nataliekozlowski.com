Robot T-Shirt Custom T-shirt design

At CodeGuard I was given the opportunity to create custom, wacky and totally original t-shirts! This t-shirt was inspired by C.G., the CodeGuard robot. He has these tentacle-like arms, so I played with that element and had his arms wrap around the shirt from front to back. We pass our shirts out at conferences and send them to customers.
